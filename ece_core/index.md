# ECE Core Courses

These are the foundational courses for the Electrical and Computer Engineering major at CMU.

| Course | Name |
|--------|------|
| [18-100](/ece_core/18100) | Introduction to Electrical and Computer Engineering |
| [18-213](/ece_core/18213) | Introduction to Computer Systems |
| [18-220](/ece_core/18220) | Electronic Devices and Analog Circuits |
| [18-240](/ece_core/18240) | Structure and Design of Digital Systems |
| [18-290](/ece_core/18290) | Signals and Systems |
| [18-500](/ece_core/18500) | ECE Design Experience |

## Recommended Sequence

1. **18-100** - First year introduction
2. **18-213** - After 15-122, covers systems programming
3. **18-220** - After 18-100, covers analog circuits
4. **18-240** - After 18-100, covers digital logic
5. **18-290** - After 18-100, covers signal processing fundamentals
6. **18-500** - Senior capstone design course
