## About This Guide

Classes at CMU can be hard. This guide gives some insight on what to expect from the core classes in the ECE and CS programs at CMU.

Each course page includes:
- **Difficulty ratings** for homework, exams, and labs
- **What to expect** from the course workload
- **How to do well** with practical study tips
- **What to watch out for** to avoid common pitfalls
- **Resources** for additional learning
