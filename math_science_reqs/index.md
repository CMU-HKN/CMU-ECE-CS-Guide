# Math & Science Requirements

These are the math and science courses required for ECE and CS majors at CMU.

| Course | Name |
|--------|------|
| [18-202](/math_science_reqs/18202) | Mathematical Foundations of Electrical Engineering |
| [21-127](/math_science_reqs/21127) | Concepts of Mathematics |
| [21-241](/math_science_reqs/21241) | Matrix Algebra |
| [21-254](/math_science_reqs/21254) | Linear Algebra and Vector Calculus for Engineers |
| [21-259](/math_science_reqs/21259) | Calculus in Three Dimensions |
| [21-260](/math_science_reqs/21260) | Differential Equations |
| [36-219](/math_science_reqs/36219) | Probability Theory and Random Processes |
| [36-225](/math_science_reqs/36225) | Introduction to Probability Theory |

## Course Notes

- **21-127 (Concepts)** is essential for 15-251 and many theoretical CS courses
- **21-241 (Matrix Algebra)** covers linear algebra fundamentals
- **21-254** combines linear algebra and vector calculus for engineers
- **36-225/36-219** cover probability - choose based on your major requirements
